# Installing via the App Store

Look for ["Paperback - A Komga Client"](https://apps.apple.com/app/paperback-a-komga-client/id1626613373) on the App Store.

Paperback is accessible in the majority of countries. You can view the complete list of available regions [here](/getting-started/installation/availability).

<img src="/assets/Renders/AppStoreBanner.png" alt="Content settings in iOS and Paperback." style="border-radius: 12px; max-width: 100%;" />

::: tip App Store Availability
If the app is not available in your country, you can temporarily change your App Store region. A guide is available [here](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT201389).
:::
